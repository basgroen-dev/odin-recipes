Pasta Alfredo

Description

Pasta Alfredo is a creamy Italian dish made with fettuccine pasta tossed in a rich sauce of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. The sauce is smooth and velvety, coating the pasta perfectly for a luxurious texture. Often served with chicken or shrimp, it's a simple yet indulgent meal that's popular for its comforting flavor and ease of preparation.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley
  • Chicken or shrimp

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic (optional) and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and stir constantly, allowing the mixture to simmer gently for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese gradually, stirring until the cheese melts and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the pan and toss it in the sauce until well coated.
  7. For an extra touch, add cooked chicken or shrimp if desired, and mix with the pasta.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot. Enjoy!
