Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's baked until golden and bubbly, resulting in a hearty, flavorful meal that's perfect for family dinners or gatherings. Variations include different fillings such as ground beef, sausage, spinach, or ricotta, making it a versatile and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Boil lasagna sheets according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté onions and garlic until softened.
  4. Add ground beef (or other meat/vegetables) and cook until browned. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes. Add chopped basil at the end.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.
  7. To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer with lasagna sheets, followed by more meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  9. Repeat the layers, finishing with a top layer of lasagna sheets and mozzarella cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
